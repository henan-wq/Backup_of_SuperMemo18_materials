To make sure you remember well, SuperMemo will test your memory. Each little test will include these steps:

  • SuperMemo will show you a question
  • You will answer the question (mentally or verbally)
  • You will click Show answer (or press Enter) to see the correct answer
  • You will compare your response with the answer displayed by SuperMemo
  • You will choose a grade reflecting how well you remember (e.g. Good if you remember well, Fail if you do not remember, etc.)
  • You will choose Next repetition (or Enter)
